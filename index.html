Pandemic

During a Pandemic

  • Public health guidelines

    Americans can continue to use and drink tap water as usual during the COVID-19 pandemic. Please be sure to follow public health guidance as the situation develops

    https://www.who.int/
  • Social Distancing

    Avoid close contact with people who are sick. When you are sick, keep your distance from others to protect them from getting sick too.

    3M 8511 Respirator, N95, Cool Flow Valve
  • Cover nose and mouth

    Cover your mouth and nose with a tissue when coughing or sneezing. It may prevent those around you from getting sick.

    https://www.amazon.com/Nitrile-Disposable-Ambidextrous-Comfortable-Industrial/dp/B085X8HXNF/ref=sr_1_6?dchild=1&keywords=latex+gloves&qid=1586390561&sr=8-6
  • Washing your hands

    Washing your hands often will help protect you from germs.

    https://www.who.int/
  • Avoid touching your eyes, nose or mouth.

    Contact the World Health Organization at 555-555-4444

    https://www.who.int/
  • Practice other good health habits

    Get plenty of sleep, be physically active, manage your stress, drink plenty of fluids, and eat nutritious food.

    https://www.who.int/
